PPE Compare

Responsive Web Design, Web Development, Brand Identity

Summary
Brand identity and responsive website for price comparison company PPE Compare. The site offers customers the ability to directly compare personal protective equipment, their prices and delivery lead times.

With a surge in PPE orders due to the current pandemic ppe-compare.com seeks to eliminate profiteering and promote competitive supplier pricing.

The website uses clear iconography, intuitively segmented content and clearly defined zones to help aid easy user navigation.
