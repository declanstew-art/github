Declan Stewart is a Graphic designer whose work spans design for print and web. He graduated with a degree in Design for Visual Communication from Croydon School of Art and has worked as a freelance designer for clients such as Lee Stafford, Croydon College and University Centre Croydon. At present he works for London based Architectural firm - Design Team.
