OBJECT!

Book Design, Visual Storytelling

Summary
Object! is a book highlighting the global problems we face as a result of over consumption and our excessive dependence on short lived or disposable items. Using everyday items to playfully illustrate scientific data, facts and future predictions, Object! seeks to communicate the unprecedented planetary emergency we currently face in an easily digestible and simplified format.
