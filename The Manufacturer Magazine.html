The Manufacturer Magazine

Magazine Design, Art Direction, Visual Journalism, Editorial Layout Design

Summary
Designing 14 issues of The Manufacturer Magazine for a 150k readership.

Over the course of 14 consecutive months I developed a clearly defined visual journalistic vocabulary. This was achieved through extensive typographic and infographic experimentation, photo retouching and editorial layout design.

It was my responsibility to manage the design schedule whilst liaising with the printer’s and communicating with clients.
