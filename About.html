Professional Bio
Declan Stewart is a London based Graphic Designer whose practice encompasses design for both print and web. He graduated with a degree in Design for Visual Communication from Croydon School of Art, and has since worked for clients such as PWC, Lee Stafford, SSE, Croydon College, Design Team and The Manufacturer magazine.

His experience spans the fields of Publishing, Events, Retail, Architecture and Education.
