Professional Bio
Declan Stewart is a London-based graphic designer whose practice spans brand strategy and design-led storytelling across both print and digital platforms. He graduated with a degree in Design for Visual Communication from the University of Sussex (CSoA) and later founded the offline advertising agency Runnel. Declan has collaborated with a wide range of clients, including PwC, NCP, THFC, Jisp, Howdens, Lee Stafford, SSE, Croydon College, and The Manufacturer magazine.
